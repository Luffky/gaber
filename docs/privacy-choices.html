如何删除数据How to delete your data

直接卸载 App。两项设置随 App 一起从设备中移除，不存在任何云端副本。 Simply uninstall the app. Both settings are removed from the device with it; no cloud copy exists anywhere.

可以拒绝哪些权限？Which permissions can you deny?

本 App 不请求任何权限，所以没有需要拒绝的。iOS 系统设置中的"画中画"（设置 → 通用 → 画中画）由你掌控：关闭后悬浮窗功能不可用，App 的其余功能不受影响。 The app never asks for a permission, so there is nothing to deny. The system-level Picture-in-Picture switch (Settings → General → Picture in Picture) is yours to control: turning it off disables the floating window while the rest of the app keeps working.
